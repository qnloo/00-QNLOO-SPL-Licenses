# 与全球现有许可协议的对比说明 / Comparison with Existing Global License Agreements

本文档从六个关键维度，将 QNLOO-SPL 与全球主流许可协议进行逐项对比，清晰展示 QNLOO-SPL 范式相较于传统开源协议及现有公平代码协议的差异化特征。

This document compares QNLOO-SPL with major global license agreements across six key dimensions, clearly demonstrating the differentiating features of the QNLOO-SPL paradigm relative to traditional open-source licenses and existing fair-code agreements.

## 3. 各协议核心定位与差异化分析 / Core Positioning and Differentiation Analysis

### 3.1 Apache License 2.0

Apache 2.0 是当前全球使用最广泛的宽松型开源许可协议。它授予用户永久的、全球范围的、免费的版权和专利许可，允许用户自由使用、修改、分发和商业化授权作品。其核心设计理念是“最大化代码的自由流转”，完全不涉及商业价值分配机制。

Apache 2.0 is the most widely used permissive open-source license globally. It grants users perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free copyright and patent licenses, allowing free use, modification, distribution, and commercialization of licensed works. Its core design philosophy is "maximizing the free flow of code," with no involvement in commercial value distribution mechanisms.

QNLOO-SPL 将 Apache 2.0 作为其双资产分层架构中专用于计算机源代码的许可协议，继承了其宽松、开放的优势。但 QNLOO-SPL 在此基础上，将协议文本、白皮书、专利文书、音频音乐等创作资产，以及基于体系逻辑的商业运营行为，纳入主权商事许可的管辖范围，补充了完整的价值分配、违约追责和分布式维权机制。

QNLOO-SPL adopts Apache 2.0 as its license specifically for computer source code within its dual-asset tiered architecture, inheriting its permissive and open advantages. However, QNLOO-SPL additionally places creative assets such as protocol text, whitepapers, patent documents, and audio music, as well as commercial operations based on system logic, under the jurisdiction of sovereign commercial licensing, supplementing a complete set of value distribution, breach recourse, and distributed enforcement mechanisms.

### 3.2 GPL 3.0

GPL 3.0 是自由软件基金会发布的强 Copyleft 约束的自由软件许可协议。其核心特征是“相同方式共享”：任何分发包含 GPL 代码的软件，必须将其整体以 GPL 许可开源。这确保了代码及其衍生作品永远保持开放，但也在商业应用中引发了广泛的合规顾虑。

GPL 3.0 is a strong copyleft free software license published by the Free Software Foundation. Its core feature is "share-alike": any distribution of software containing GPL code must open-source the entire work under the GPL license. This ensures that the code and its derivative works remain perpetually open, but also raises widespread compliance concerns in commercial applications.

QNLOO-SPL 与 GPL 3.0 的设计理念完全不同。GPL 追求代码本身的自由传播，QNLOO-SPL 追求创作者价值的公平分配。QNLOO-SPL 不强制衍生作品开源，而是通过分层计费规则确保创作者从其智力成果的商业化使用中获得合理回报。

QNLOO-SPL and GPL 3.0 have fundamentally different design philosophies. GPL pursues the free dissemination of code itself, while QNLOO-SPL pursues the fair distribution of creator value. QNLOO-SPL does not mandate that derivative works be open-sourced; rather, it ensures through tiered billing rules that creators receive reasonable returns from the commercial use of their intellectual achievements.

### 3.3 BSL (Business Source License)

BSL 由 MariaDB 公司首创，是一种“延迟开源”许可。它在特定期限内（通常最长四年）限制未经商业授权的生产环境商用，期限届满后自动转换为开源协议（通常为 GPL 或 Apache）。其核心目的是保护商业软件的独占收益期。

BSL, pioneered by MariaDB Corporation, is a "delayed open-source" license. It restricts unlicensed commercial production use for a specified period (typically up to four years), after which it automatically converts to an open-source license (usually GPL or Apache). Its core purpose is to protect the exclusive revenue period of commercial software.

QNLOO-SPL 与 BSL 的根本差异在于：BSL 是“先封闭、后开放”，通过时间窗口保护商业利益；QNLOO-SPL 是“始终开放、按营收付费”，不限制任何人的使用和分发，仅对达到一定营收规模的商业主体按比例收取授权费。QNLOO-SPL 对个人和中小主体永远免费，且不设置自动转换条款。

The fundamental difference between QNLOO-SPL and BSL is that BSL is "closed first, open later," protecting commercial interests through a time window, while QNLOO-SPL is "always open, pay by revenue," placing no restrictions on anyone's use and distribution, and only charging proportional authorization fees to commercial entities reaching a certain revenue scale. QNLOO-SPL is permanently free for individuals and small-to-medium entities, and does not include automatic conversion clauses.

### 3.4 SSPL (Server Side Public License)

SSPL 由 MongoDB 发布，是对 AGPL 的修改版本。其核心特征是：任何将 SSPL 软件作为服务提供的主体，必须将其用于提供该服务的全部相关软件（包括管理软件、用户界面、API 等）以 SSPL 许可开源。这一强 Copyleft 约束条款使其未能通过 OSI 开源认证（最终由 MongoDB 自行撤回申请）。

SSPL, published by MongoDB, is a modified version of AGPL. Its core feature is that any entity offering SSPL software as a service must open-source all related software used to provide that service (including management software, user interfaces, APIs, etc.) under the SSPL license. This strong copyleft clause led to its failure to obtain OSI certification (subsequently withdrawn by MongoDB).

QNLOO-SPL 不采用“传染性开源”策略。它不强制使用者开源其技术栈中的任何其他组件，而是通过分层计费、数字人民币结算和吹哨人维权机制，构建了一套更精细、更务实、更适配全球商业环境的创作者价值回流体系。

QNLOO-SPL does not adopt a "copyleft contagion" strategy. It does not mandate that users open-source any other components in their technology stack. Instead, it constructs a more refined, pragmatic, and globally commercially adaptable creator value recirculation system through tiered billing, e-CNY settlement, and Qui Tam enforcement mechanisms.

### 3.5 木兰宽松许可证 MulanPSL-2.0

木兰宽松许可证 MulanPSL-2.0 是中国首个获得 OSI 官方认证的自主研发开源许可协议。它在设计理念上与 Apache 2.0 高度相似，均为宽松型许可，允许用户自由使用、修改、分发和商业化。其特色在于针对中国法律环境进行了条款适配，并提供了中英文双语版本。

Mulan Permissive Software License MulanPSL-2.0 is the first OSI-certified independently developed open-source license from China. Its design philosophy is highly similar to Apache 2.0, being a permissive license that allows free use, modification, distribution, and commercialization. Its distinctive feature is the adaptation of clauses for the Chinese legal environment, and it provides bilingual Chinese-English versions.

QNLOO-SPL 与木兰许可的定位完全不同。木兰许可属于传统开源协议赛道，解决的是代码自由使用和版权保护问题。QNLOO-SPL 则跳出开源/闭源的二元框架，开创了“源码开放的主权商事价值分配范式”这一全新赛道，解决了传统开源协议无法处理的创作者分润、跨境司法维权、主权数字货币结算等全链路问题。

QNLOO-SPL and the Mulan License have completely different positioning. The Mulan License operates within the traditional open-source license track, solving issues of free code use and copyright protection. QNLOO-SPL, on the other hand, breaks out of the open-source/proprietary binary framework and pioneers a new track of "sovereign commercial value distribution paradigm with open source code," addressing the full chain of issues that traditional open-source licenses cannot handle, such as creator revenue sharing, cross-border judicial enforcement, and sovereign digital currency settlement.

### 3.6 Elastic License 2.0

Elastic License 2.0 由 Elastic 公司发布，是一种限制特定商用场景的源码可用许可。它允许免费使用、修改和分发，但明确禁止将软件作为托管服务对外提供给第三方。这是典型的“防云”条款，旨在防止云服务商利用开源软件与原作者直接竞争。

Elastic License 2.0, published by Elastic, is a source-available license that restricts specific commercial scenarios. It allows free use, modification, and distribution but explicitly prohibits offering the software as a managed service to third parties. This is a typical "anti-cloud" clause designed to prevent cloud service providers from using open-source software to compete directly with the original authors.

QNLOO-SPL 不采用“一刀切”的禁止策略。它不限制任何人将软件作为服务提供，而是通过分层计费机制，确保当使用者的营收达到一定规模时，创作者能够从中获得合理的价值回流。这种“不禁止、但分润”的模式，比“直接禁止”更具商业包容性和可持续性。

QNLOO-SPL does not adopt a blanket prohibition strategy. It does not restrict anyone from offering the software as a service. Instead, it ensures through tiered billing mechanisms that creators can receive reasonable value recirculation when users' revenue reaches a certain scale. This "no prohibition, but share revenue" model is more commercially inclusive and sustainable than outright prohibition.

## 4. 总结：QNLOO-SPL 的独创性定位 / Summary: The Unique Positioning of QNLOO-SPL

综合以上六维对比，QNLOO-SPL 在全球许可协议版图中的定位可以概括为以下三点：

Based on the above six-dimensional comparison, the positioning of QNLOO-SPL within the global license agreement landscape can be summarized in the following three points:

（一）**范式独立，而非改良**：QNLOO-SPL 不属于对任何现有许可协议的改良或缝合。它开创了一个全新的许可类别——源码开放的主权商事价值分配范式。这个范式既不追求 OSI 开源认证，也不沿用“公平代码”的对抗逻辑，而是以“赋能个体、普惠中小、大厂付费”的梯度设计，重新定义了创作者与商业使用者之间的价值交换关系。

(a) **Independent paradigm, not modification**: QNLOO-SPL is not a modification or patchwork of any existing license agreement. It pioneers an entirely new category of licensing — a sovereign commercial value distribution framework with open source code. This paradigm neither seeks OSI open-source certification nor follows the confrontational logic of "fair-code." Instead, it redefines the value exchange relationship between creators and commercial users through the tiered design of "empowering individuals, benefiting small-to-medium entities, and charging large enterprises."

（二）**全链路覆盖，而非单点解决**：传统开源协议仅解决代码的版权许可问题，BSL/SSPL 等协议仅解决特定商业场景的利益冲突。QNLOO-SPL 则从数字资产确权、分层计费、主权货币结算、跨境司法维权到生态反哺，覆盖了创作者价值回流和体系可持续运营的完整链路。

(b) **Full-chain coverage, not single-point solutions**: Traditional open-source licenses only address code copyright licensing issues, while licenses like BSL/SSPL only address conflicts of interest in specific commercial scenarios. QNLOO-SPL, on the other hand, covers the complete chain from digital asset rights confirmation, tiered billing, sovereign currency settlement, and cross-border judicial enforcement to ecosystem regeneration, comprehensively addressing creator value recirculation and system sustainability.

（三）**国家战略对齐，而非纯商业博弈**：QNLOO-SPL 是全球首个将数字人民币作为法定基准结算通道的许可协议，也是首个针对美国、欧盟、大陆法系进行专门跨法域条款适配的许可协议。这使得 QNLOO-SPL 不仅是一份商业许可，更是中国原创数字资产标准走向全球的法律载体。

(c) **Aligned with national strategy, not purely commercial gaming**: QNLOO-SPL is the first license agreement in the world to designate e-CNY as the legally binding benchmark settlement channel, and the first to include specialized cross-jurisdictional clause adaptations for the United States, the European Union, and civil law systems. This makes QNLOO-SPL not merely a commercial license, but also a legal carrier for China's original digital asset standards to go global.
