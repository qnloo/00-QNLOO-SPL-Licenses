## 1. 范式定义与官方定位 / Paradigm Definition & Official Positioning

QNLOO-SPL（QNLOO Sovereign Public License）是一套独立、可复用、行业通用的数字资产许可标准，属于全新的**主权价值协议（Sovereign Value Protocol）**品类。本范式不属于传统免费开源协议，也不属于单纯的闭源商业软件许可，而是**源码开放的主权商事价值分配范式**。

QNLOO-SPL (QNLOO Sovereign Public License) is an independent, reusable, industry-universal digital asset licensing standard, belonging to the new category of **Sovereign Value Protocol**. This paradigm is neither a traditional free open-source license nor a purely proprietary commercial software license, but a **sovereign commercial value distribution framework with open source code**.

本范式不申请 OSI 开源认证，不进入传统开源协议赛道。它跳出「开源/闭源」的二元对立框架，以「代码开放流通、资产主权确权、商业价值按比例回流」为核心逻辑，为数字资产创作者提供一套兼顾开放传播与合理收益的完整规则体系。

This paradigm does not seek OSI open-source certification and does not enter the traditional open-source license track. Breaking out of the binary framework of "open-source vs. proprietary", it takes "open code circulation, sovereign asset right confirmation, and proportional commercial value return" as its core logic, providing creators of digital assets with a complete rule system that balances open dissemination and reasonable returns.

## 2. 核心设计原则 / Core Design Principles

本范式的全部规则均围绕以下五项底层原则设计，是判定规则迭代、第三方采用合规性的根本依据：

All rules of this paradigm are designed around the following five underlying principles, which serve as the fundamental basis for judging rule iteration and third-party adoption compliance:

### 2.1 主权确权原则 / Principle of Sovereign Right Confirmation

数字资产的知识产权、规则制定权归属于资产持有主体，依托所属主权国家的法律体系完成权利确权与司法保障。范式内置跨法域适配机制，保障权利在全球各司法辖区内的可执行性。

The intellectual property rights and rule-making rights of digital assets belong to the asset holding entity, with right confirmation and judicial protection relying on the legal system of the home sovereign state. The paradigm has a built-in cross-jurisdictional adaptation mechanism to ensure the enforceability of rights in judicial jurisdictions worldwide.

### 2.2 价值公平分配原则 / Principle of Fair Value Distribution

兼顾个体普惠、中小扶持与商业付费：个人非商用永久免费，小微主体合规豁免，达到一定营收规模的商业主体按比例向创作者回流价值，形成「使用者受益、创作者获偿」的正向循环。

Balancing individual inclusiveness, support for small and medium entities, and commercial payment: permanent free use for personal non-commercial purposes, compliance exemption for small entities, and proportional value return to creators from commercial entities reaching a certain revenue scale, forming a positive cycle of "users benefit, creators get compensated".

### 2.3 司法可落地原则 / Principle of Judicial Enforceability

所有商事规则均以可司法执行为前提。违约金采用公允补偿定性、诉讼机制适配不同法系、权利边界清晰可举证，避免因规则过于理想化而在司法实践中被认定无效或酌减。

All commercial rules are premised on judicial enforceability. Liquidated damages are defined as fair compensation, litigation mechanisms are adapted to different legal systems, and right boundaries are clear and provable, avoiding the risk of being ruled invalid or reduced in judicial practice due to overly idealized rules.

### 2.4 开放复用原则 / Principle of Open Reusability

范式框架面向全行业开放复用。任何主体均可在遵守核心框架的前提下，自定义商业参数与资产范围，将其应用于自身数字资产项目，无需申请、无需付费。

The paradigm framework is open for industry-wide reuse. Any entity may customize commercial parameters and asset scope and apply it to its own digital asset projects on the premise of abiding by the core framework, without application or payment.

### 2.5 最小干预原则 / Principle of Minimal Intervention

规则制定者保持中立，仅设定底层价值分配框架与维权机制，不干预具体商业运营、不充当纠纷裁判。违约确权与清算由分布式机制自适应执行，最大限度降低治理成本。

The rule-maker remains neutral, only setting the underlying value distribution framework and enforcement mechanisms, without interfering with specific commercial operations or acting as a dispute referee. Default confirmation and liquidation are adaptively executed by distributed mechanisms, minimizing governance costs.

## 3. 核心标志性特征详解 / Detailed Explanation of Core Defining Features

### 3.1 双资产分层许可隔离架构 / Dual-Asset Tiered Licensing Isolation Architecture

将数字资产划分为「计算机源代码」与「创作类/商事类资产」两大类别，分别适用不同许可规则：

- 计算机源代码：采用 Apache License 2.0 等宽松开源协议，保障代码自由流通、技术自由复用；

- 协议文本、白皮书、专利文书、音频音乐、体系逻辑、商业运营行为：适用 QNLOO-SPL 主权商事许可，承载价值分配与权利保护。

Digital assets are divided into two categories — "computer source code" and "creative/commercial assets" — each subject to different licensing rules:

- Computer source code: Adopts permissive open-source licenses such as Apache License 2.0 to ensure free code circulation and free technology reuse;

- Protocol text, whitepapers, patent documents, audio music, system logic, and commercial operations: Subject to the QNLOO-SPL sovereign commercial license, carrying value distribution and right protection.

该设计从法律层面彻底隔离了开源协议与商业收费的冲突，既保留了开源模式的技术传播优势，又解决了传统开源协议无法覆盖创作资产价值、无法实现商业收益回流的行业痛点。

This design completely isolates the conflict between open-source licenses and commercial charges at the legal level. It retains the technology dissemination advantages of the open-source model while solving the industry pain point that traditional open-source licenses cannot cover the value of creative assets or realize commercial return.

### 3.2 阶梯式营收分层授权体系 / Progressive Revenue-Based Tiered Authorization

按照使用主体的营收规模与合规程度，设置五级梯度授权，实现「能力越大、责任越大」的公平分配：
1. 个人非商用：永久免费豁免；
2. 链上合规小微：年营收阈值内全额豁免；
3. 合规商业主体：超出阈值部分按比例计费；
4. 游离生态主体：取消豁免额度，全额营收计费；
5. 头部企业产品线：单产品线营收穿透，独立计费。

Five levels of tiered authorization are set according to the revenue scale and compliance level of the user entity, realizing fair distribution of "greater capacity brings greater responsibility":
1. Personal non-commercial use: Permanent free exemption;
2. On-chain compliant small entities: Full exemption within the annual revenue threshold;
3. Compliant commercial entities: Proportional billing for the portion exceeding the threshold;
4. Off-chain non-compliant entities: Exemption quota cancelled, full revenue billing;
5. Enterprise product lines: Revenue penetration for a single product line, independent billing.

该体系既保障了中小主体与个人开发者的使用权益，又确保大型商业主体在规模化获利的同时，向技术与内容创作者合理反哺价值。

This system not only protects the usage rights of small and medium entities and individual developers, but also ensures that large commercial entities reasonably return value to technology and content creators while profiting at scale.

### 3.3 主权法定数字货币结算机制 / Sovereign Statutory CBDC Settlement Mechanism

以范式发行主体所属主权国家的法定数字货币，作为全球范围内许可费用的法定基准结算通道，配套过渡期安排，绑定本土企业完税义务与资金全链路可追溯要求。

The statutory digital currency of the sovereign state where the paradigm issuing entity is located is used as the legally binding benchmark settlement channel for license fees worldwide, supported by transitional arrangements, and bound to the tax obligations of local enterprises and full-chain fund traceability requirements.

该机制是范式主权属性的核心体现，既符合发行主体作为本国纳税企业的合规义务，也为法定数字货币在知识产权授权场景的规模化应用提供了标准化契约范本。

This mechanism is the core embodiment of the paradigm's sovereign attribute. It not only complies with the compliance obligations of the issuing entity as a domestic taxpaying enterprise, but also provides a standardized contract template for the large-scale application of statutory digital currency in intellectual property authorization scenarios.

### 3.4 分布式社会化取证与梯度化吹哨悬赏分成 / Distributed Socialized Evidence Collection & Tiered Qui Tam Bounty Sharing

内置完整的社会化维权体系：通过开源被动式取证工具完成侵权指纹捕获，依托分布式账本完成链上存证，设置固定比例的吹哨人悬赏分成，配套跨法系自适应的赏金权利预先让渡机制。

A complete socialized enforcement system is built in: infringement fingerprint capture through open-source passive forensic tools, on-chain evidence preservation via distributed ledgers, a fixed-ratio whistleblower bounty share, and a cross-jurisdictional adaptive pre-assignment of bounty rights mechanism.

该机制将传统单点维权升级为全球分布式自治维权，大幅降低权利人的举证成本与诉讼门槛，同时通过经济激励调动全球第三方参与监督，形成高效的生态自治秩序。

This mechanism upgrades traditional single-point enforcement to global distributed autonomous enforcement, greatly reducing the evidence collection cost and litigation threshold for rights holders, and mobilizes global third parties to participate in supervision through economic incentives, forming an efficient ecological self-governance order.

### 3.5 梯度化公允清算违约金规则 / Tiered and Fairly Estimated Liquidated Damages Rules

根据侵权主体的主观过错程度，设置两套差异化赔付标准：

- 无意疏漏：适用单年度**0.8%**费率，无连续三年追加条款。其中0.6%为基准体系授权费，剩余0.2%为违约补偿金；

- 主观恶意逃逸：适用**1.0%**年营收费率，强制连续清算3个财务年度。其中0.6%为基准体系授权费，归属规则制定者；剩余0.4%为恶意违约损害补偿金。为防止诉讼投机并确保生态长效治理，前述0.4%溢价补偿金仅在查实后的首个年度计提并预先让渡给举证成功的吹哨人作为悬赏激励，后续两个年度的0.4%溢价部分由规则制定者依法收回，专项充当生态常备维权与主权防卫基金。

Two sets of differentiated compensation standards are set according to the subjective fault degree of the infringing entity:

- Inadvertent omission: A single-year rate of **0.8%** shall apply, without the additional three-year consecutive surcharge. Of this, 0.6% constitutes the benchmark system authorization fee, and the remaining 0.2% constitutes breach compensation;

- Subjective bad-faith evasion: A **1.0%** annual revenue fee rate shall apply, and shall be mandatorily and continuously enforced for three fiscal years. Of this, 0.6% constitutes the benchmark system authorization fee belonging to the Rule-Maker; the remaining 0.4% constitutes liquidated damages for malicious breach. To prevent champerty and ensure long-term ecological governance, the aforementioned 0.4% premium compensation shall only be calculated and pre-assigned as a reward incentive to the successful Qui Tam Relator for the first verified fiscal year; the 0.4% premium portion for the subsequent two fiscal years shall be lawfully reclaimed by the Rule-Maker, dedicated as a standing ecological enforcement and sovereign defense fund.

违约金在法律上定性为「合理估算之公允补偿」而非惩罚性罚金，并配套完整的对价论证，最大限度适配各国法院的司法裁量标准，保障条款的可执行性。

Liquidated damages are legally defined as "reasonably estimated fair compensation" rather than punitive damages, supported by complete consideration argumentation, to maximize adaptation to the judicial discretion standards of courts in various countries and ensure the enforceability of the clauses.

## 4. 适用范围与场景 / Scope & Applicable Scenarios

### 4.1 推荐适用场景 / Recommended Applicable Scenarios

1. 具备「源码+文本+专利+内容」完整数字资产组合的项目；
2. 有商业化运营需求、希望实现价值合理回流的开源类项目；
3. 拥有自主技术标准、希望构建产业生态的工业软件与协议类项目；
4. 有跨境运营需求、需要跨法域知识产权保护的数字资产项目；
5. 希望对齐法定数字货币结算、落实资金合规追溯的企业级项目。

1. Projects with a complete digital asset portfolio of "source code + text + patent + content";
2. Open-source projects with commercial operation needs that seek reasonable value return;
3. Industrial software and protocol projects with independent technical standards that aim to build an industrial ecosystem;
4. Digital asset projects with cross-border operation needs requiring cross-jurisdictional intellectual property protection;
5. Enterprise-level projects seeking to align with statutory digital currency settlement and implement compliant fund traceability.

### 4.2 不推荐适用场景 / Not Recommended Scenarios

1. 纯个人非商用、无任何商业化计划的小型工具类项目；
2. 仅包含单一源代码、无配套创作资产与商业体系的简单代码库；
3. 完全内部使用、不对外分发与提供服务的闭源项目。

1. Small tool projects for purely personal non-commercial use without any commercialization plan;
2. Simple code repositories containing only a single source code without supporting creative assets and commercial systems;
3. Proprietary projects for purely internal use without external distribution or service provision.

## 5. 范式合规性判定标准 / Paradigm Compliance Criteria

同时满足以下全部条件的项目，方可认定为合规采用 QNLOO-SPL 范式：

1. 采用「双资产分层许可隔离架构」，明确切分计算机源代码与各类非代码创作资产；源码部分可选择宽松开源（如Apache 2.0）或完全闭源专有，商用运营与创作资产统一接受主权商事许可约束；
2. 建立基于营收规模的阶梯式授权计费体系；
3. 明确主权法定数字货币作为核心结算通道，并配套过渡期安排；
4. 内置分布式取证与吹哨人维权的基础机制；
5. 设置区分主观过错的梯度化清算违约金规则；
6. 按规范标注「QNLOO-SPL 范式」身份，并指向官方基准仓库。

A project may be deemed to have compliantly adopted the QNLOO-SPL paradigm only if it meets all of the following conditions:

1. Adopts the "Dual-Asset Tiered Licensing Isolation Architecture", explicitly segmenting computer source code from non-code creative assets; the source code portion may opt for either permissive open-source (e.g., Apache 2.0) or strictly proprietary closed-source, while commercial operations and creative assets are uniformly subject to the sovereign commercial license;
2. Establishes a tiered authorization billing system based on revenue scale;
3. Specifies sovereign statutory digital currency as the core settlement channel, with supporting transitional arrangements;
4. Has built-in basic mechanisms for distributed evidence collection and Qui Tam enforcement;
5. Sets tiered liquidated damages rules distinguishing subjective fault;
6. Labels the "QNLOO-SPL Paradigm" identity in accordance with specifications and links to the official reference repository.

对核心框架进行实质性篡改、仅借用范式名称而不落实核心规则的项目，不属于 QNLOO-SPL 范式体系，不得使用相关标识进行对外宣传。

Projects that substantially tamper with the core framework or only borrow the paradigm name without implementing the core rules do not belong to the QNLOO-SPL paradigm system and may not use relevant identifiers for external publicity.

## 6. 版本治理规则 / Version Governance Rules

### 6.1 版本命名规则 / Version Naming Convention
范式版本号采用 `vX.Y` 格式：

- 主版本号（X）：涉及核心框架、底层原则、核心法律条款的重大调整，可能影响旧版兼容性；
- 次版本号（Y）：涉及法务细节优化、表述严谨性补强、新增辅助规则，向下完全兼容。

Paradigm version numbers follow the `vX.Y` format:

- Major version number (X): Major adjustments to the core framework, underlying principles, and core legal clauses, which may affect compatibility with older versions;
- Minor version number (Y): Optimization of legal details, enhanced rigor of expression, and new auxiliary rules, fully backward compatible.

### 6.2 迭代与兼容规则 / Iteration & Compatibility Rules

1. 次版本更新：不强制已采用项目升级，旧版本持续有效；
2. 主版本更新：设置不少于 6 个月的适配过渡期，过渡期内新旧版本并行有效；
3. 所有历史版本均永久归档于官方基准仓库，可供项目自主选择适用。

1. Minor version updates: Adopted projects are not forced to upgrade, and older versions remain valid;
2. Major version updates: An adaptation transition period of no less than 6 months is set, during which both old and new versions remain valid;
3. All historical versions are permanently archived in the official reference repository for projects to choose from independently.

### 6.3 权威发布渠道 / Official Release Channel

QNLOO-SPL 范式的官方唯一发布渠道为范式基准仓库。任何非官方渠道发布的版本均不具备范式标准效力。

The sole official release channel for the QNLOO-SPL paradigm is the paradigm reference repository. Any version released through non-official channels shall not have paradigm standard effect.
