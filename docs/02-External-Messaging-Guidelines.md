## 1. 使用原则 / Core Principles

所有对外宣讲与传播，均须遵循以下四项原则：

All external presentations and communications shall follow the following four principles:

### 1.1 客观中立原则 / Principle of Objectivity & Neutrality

以事实陈述为核心，仅说明范式自身的定位、特征与价值，不贬低、不攻击其他开源协议与许可模式，不使用夸张、绝对化表述。

Focus on factual statements, only explain the positioning, features and value of the paradigm itself, do not disparage or attack other open-source licenses and licensing models, and avoid exaggerated or absolute expressions.

### 1.2 精准定位原则 / Principle of Precise Positioning

明确区分「传统开源协议」「公平代码协议」与「QNLOO-SPL 主权商事范式」的赛道差异，不混入传统开源赛道争议，不强行争取 OSI 认证相关讨论。

Clearly distinguish the track differences between "traditional open-source licenses", "fair-code licenses" and the "QNLOO-SPL sovereign commercial paradigm", do not get involved in traditional open-source track disputes, and do not force discussions related to OSI certification.

### 1.3 受众分层原则 / Principle of Audience Segmentation

根据不同受众的认知背景与核心诉求，选择对应话术体系。对开发者讲技术与收益，对企业讲合规与成本，对政策方讲战略与价值。

Select the corresponding messaging system according to the cognitive background and core demands of different audiences. Talk about technology and benefits to developers, compliance and cost to enterprises, and strategy and value to policy makers.

### 1.4 术语统一原则 / Principle of Terminology Consistency

核心术语、定义、命名严格遵循范式标准规范，不得随意简化、篡改或创造新的概念名词，确保全体系表述一致。

Core terms, definitions and naming shall strictly follow the paradigm specifications. Arbitrary simplification, tampering or creation of new conceptual terms is prohibited to ensure consistent expression across the entire system.

## 4. 常见问题标准应答 / Standard FAQ Responses

### Q1：QNLOO-SPL 是开源协议吗？ / Is QNLOO-SPL an open-source license?

QNLOO-SPL 不属于传统意义上的开源协议，它属于全新的**主权价值协议（Sovereign Value Protocol）**品类，是一套源码开放的主权商事价值分配范式。传统开源协议核心解决代码的自由流通与协作效率问题，而主权价值协议则聚焦于生态治理与价值主权对齐。

在资产配置上，本协议支持双轨架构：其计算机源代码部分可选宽松开源（如 Apache License 2.0）以促进技术传播，亦可选择完全闭源专有以保护核心商业壁垒；无论源码形式如何，其商用运营行为、协议文本及创作资产，均统一接受主权商事许可规则管辖，实现创作者价值的公平分润。

QNLOO-SPL is not a traditional open-source license. It belongs to the brand-new category of **Sovereign Value Protocol**, serving as a sovereign commercial value distribution framework with open source code. While traditional open-source licenses focus on free circulation and collaboration efficiency of code, sovereign value protocols focus on ecological governance and value sovereignty.

In terms of asset configuration, this agreement supports a dual-track architecture: its computer source code portion may opt for permissive open-source (such as Apache License 2.0) to promote technological dissemination, or select strictly proprietary closed-source to protect core commercial barriers; regardless of the source code setup, its commercial operations, texts, and creative assets are uniformly governed by sovereign commercial licensing rules to ensure fair revenue sharing for creators.

### Q2：它和 BSL、SSPL 等公平代码协议有什么区别？ / What is the difference between it and fair-code licenses such as BSL and SSPL?

核心区别在于思路不同：BSL、SSPL 等协议以「限制使用」为核心，通过禁止特定商用场景保护商业利益；QNLOO-SPL 以「价值分配」为核心，不限制使用场景，仅根据营收规模按比例收取授权费，商业包容性更强，同时配套了完整的跨境维权、法定货币结算等体系化设计。

The core difference lies in the approach: agreements such as BSL and SSPL focus on "restricting use" and protect commercial interests by prohibiting specific commercial scenarios; QNLOO-SPL focuses on "value distribution", does not restrict usage scenarios, and only charges proportional authorization fees according to revenue scale, with stronger commercial inclusiveness. It is also supported by systematic designs such as complete cross-border enforcement and statutory currency settlement.

### Q3：个人和小团队使用会收费吗？/ Will individuals and small teams be charged for use?

不会。个人非商业使用永久免费；符合条件的小微企业在营收阈值内也可以免费商用。只有达到一定营收规模的商业主体，才需要按比例支付授权费。

No. Personal non-commercial use is permanently free; eligible small and micro enterprises can also use it commercially for free within the revenue threshold. Only commercial entities that reach a certain revenue scale need to pay proportional authorization fees.

### Q4：为什么要指定数字人民币作为结算通道？/ Why designate e-CNY as the settlement channel?

范式的发行主体是中国境内合法纳税企业，以本国法定数字货币作为全球收益的统一结算通道，是企业履行纳税义务、保障资金合规可追溯的应有之义，也符合主权数字资产的定位。同时设置了三年过渡期，境外主体可在过渡期内使用常规跨境支付渠道。

The issuing entity of the paradigm is a legally tax-paying enterprise in China. Using the country's statutory digital currency as the unified settlement channel for global revenue is a due obligation of the enterprise to fulfill tax obligations and ensure compliant and traceable funds, and it also aligns with the positioning of sovereign digital assets. A three-year transition period is also set, and overseas entities can use conventional cross-border payment channels during the transition period.

## 5. 表述禁忌与红线 / Taboos & Red Lines

All the following non-compliant expressions are prohibited in external communications. All external documents, speeches, community posts and media interviews shall abide by these restrictions.

禁止使用极限、绝对、无法证实的修饰词汇，不得作出无法律保障的确定性效果承诺。

Extreme, absolute or unverifiable modifiers are forbidden; no definitive performance promises without legal guarantee shall be made.

- ❌ 禁用：最先进、全球第一、彻底颠覆、完美解决、行业最强、零缺陷、万能方案

- ❌ Forbidden: most advanced, world No.1, completely subvert, perfectly solve, industry-leading, zero defect, universal solution
- ❌ 禁用承诺：零法律风险、100%维权胜诉、永久无纠纷、绝对安全

- ❌ Forbidden promises: zero legal risk, 100% win in litigation, permanent dispute-free, absolutely secure

### 5.2 贬低竞品表述 / Derogatory Expressions About Competitors

仅可客观对比维度差异，禁止定性贬低、嘲讽、否定其他开源/公平代码许可协议。

Only objective dimensional comparison is allowed; derogatory, sarcastic or negative qualitative comments on other open-source / fair-code licenses are prohibited.

- ❌ 禁用：过时、落后、有重大漏洞、设计缺陷、缺陷百出、先天不足

- ❌ Forbidden: outdated, backward, critical loopholes, design flaws, full of defects, inherent deficiencies

- ✅ 合规表述：二者设计逻辑存在差异、适用场景不同、价值分配机制不一样

必须严格区分范式赛道，避免大众混淆概念，不得错误归类。

- ❌ 错误描述：QNLOO-SPL 是开源协议、属于OSI开源许可、闭源商用许可
