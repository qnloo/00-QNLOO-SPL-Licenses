## 1. 版本命名规则 / Version Naming Convention

范式版本号采用 `vX.Y` 语义化格式，规则如下：

* **主版本号（X）**：涉及核心框架、底层原则、核心法律条款的重大调整，可能破坏旧版本兼容性。
* **次版本号（Y）**：涉及法务表述优化、规则细节补全、新增辅助机制与释义文档，向下完全兼容。

The paradigm version number follows the `vX.Y` semantic format, with the following rules:

* **Major Version (X)**: Major adjustments to the core framework, underlying principles and core legal clauses, which may break backward compatibility with older versions.
* **Minor Version (Y)**: Optimization of legal wording, completion of rule details, addition of auxiliary mechanisms and interpretation documents, fully backward compatible.

## 3. 版本迭代管理规则 / Version Iteration Governance Rules

### 3.1 发布渠道 / Release Channel

QNLOO-SPL 范式所有正式版本的唯一官方发布渠道为 QNLOO-SPL 官方基准仓库。任何非官方渠道发布的版本均不具备标准效力。

The sole official release channel for all official versions of the QNLOO-SPL paradigm is the official QNLOO-SPL reference repository. Any version released through non-official channels shall have no standard effect.

### 3.2 适配过渡期 / Adaptation Transition Period

* **次版本更新（Y）**：不强制已采用项目升级，旧版本持续有效，无强制过渡期要求。

* **主版本更新（X）**：设置不少于 6 个月的强制适配过渡期，过渡期内新旧版本并行有效；过渡期结束后，新增商用项目须采用新版本。


* **Minor Version Updates (Y)**: Adopted projects are not forced to upgrade, older versions remain valid, and no mandatory transition period is required.

* **Major Version Updates (X)**: A mandatory adaptation transition period of no less than 6 months is set, during which both old and new versions remain valid; after the transition period, new commercial projects must adopt the new version.

### 3.3 历史版本归档 / Historical Version Archiving

所有失效或被替代的历史版本，均完整移入官方基准仓库的 `archive/` 目录留存，标注版本号与失效日期，永久可查、可供追溯。

All invalid or superseded historical versions shall be fully moved to the `archive/` directory of the official reference repository for retention, marked with version number and expiration date, permanently accessible and traceable.

### 3.4 版本效力说明 / Version Effectiveness Notes

已采用特定版本的项目，可自主选择是否升级至新版本；未升级的项目继续适用其原版本条款，版本权利义务不受后续迭代影响。

Projects that have adopted a specific version may independently choose whether to upgrade to a new version; projects that do not upgrade shall continue to be subject to the clauses of their original version, and their version rights and obligations shall not be affected by subsequent iterations.
