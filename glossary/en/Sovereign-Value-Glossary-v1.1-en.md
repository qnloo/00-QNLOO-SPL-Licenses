## II. Chapter-by-Chapter Core Terms

### Foreword

1. **Axiom of Value Exchange (价值交换公理)** ***Jia Zhi Jiao Huan Gong Li*** [Jyah · Jrr · Jyow · Hwahn · Gung · Lee]
   The first universal axiom of the QNLOO system: Every sustainable positive outcome necessarily arises from creating perceptible real value for relevant parties. Unidirectional value flows will eventually collapse. **In the context of the Sovereign Value Protocol, it specifically refers to** repairing the break in value exchange under the AI-era open-source model — where traditional open-source exchanged commercial revenue for dissemination efficiency, while in the AI era, cloud vendors and training clusters directly capture creator value. This protocol reconstructs a two-way value cycle between creators, developers and commercial users through a dual-track system of "one-time knowledge base buyouts under confirmed-rights deterrence" and "continuous revenue sharing for commercial technology / vertical snapshots."

2. **Axiom of Uncertainty (不确定性公理)** ***Bu Que Ding Xing Gong Li*** [Boo · Chweh · Ding · Shing · Gung · Lee]
   The second universal axiom of the QNLOO system: The future and the external environment are inherently uncertain. Only by anchoring to foundational rights cornerstones and deterministic infrastructure can uncertainty be resolved into predictable certainty. **In the context of the Sovereign Value Protocol, it specifically refers to** resolving the uncertainty of global compliance — where cross-border enforcement faces the compounded uncertainties of jurisdictional differences, high evidence-collection barriers, and enforcement difficulties. This protocol does not rely on the cross-border capacity of a single entity but anchors to the judicial jurisdiction, statutory currency settlement and tax administration systems of each sovereign state, transforming cross-border compliance into localized distributed judicial implementation.

3. **Axiom of Cognitive Cost (认知成本公理)** ***Ren Zhi Cheng Ben Gong Li*** [Run · Jrr · Chung · Bun · Gung · Lee]
   The third universal axiom of the QNLOO system: Human time, energy and computational power are all finite resources. One must verify maximum uncertainty at minimum cost. **In the context of the Sovereign Value Protocol, it specifically refers to** compressing the participation costs of all parties to near zero — developers can complete compliance deployment by replacing files, rights holders advance no enforcement costs, and relators participate in oversight with zero barriers through open-source forensic toolkits, enabling the system to self-operate in the lowest-friction state.

4. **Binary Framework (二元框架)** ***Er Yuan Kuang Jia*** [Ar · Ywen · Kwong · Jyah]
   The cognitive coordinate system in which the current digital asset licensing domain recognizes only two options: "open-source" and "closed-source." It forces creators into an either-or dilemma — open-source forfeits commercial returns for ecosystem diffusion, while closed-source forfeits ecosystem diffusion for revenue certainty. In the AI era, the value of open-source is unidirectionally captured by cloud vendors, while the barriers of closed-source are breached by the reverse-engineering capabilities of large models. Both options within the binary framework are heading toward structural failure.

5. **Sovereign Value Protocol (SVP, 主权价值协议)** ***Zhu Quan Jia Zhi Xie Yi*** [Joo · Chwen · Jyah · Jrr · Shyeh · Yee]
   A third category of digital asset licensing that stands parallel to open-source and proprietary commercial licenses. It breaks free from the inherent limitations of the copyright system, which only regulates "copying, modification and distribution," by taking the judicial, monetary and taxation systems of sovereign states as its core anchors, and constructing a full-chain mechanism for commercial value recirculation. Its core logic is "autonomous decision on open or closed source, substantive sovereign right confirmation, and dual-track value recirculation (including one-time buyouts and continuous proportional revenue sharing)." QNLOO-SPL is the first official implementation paradigm of this new category, open for industry-wide reuse.

6. **QNLOO-SPL (主权价值协议范式)** ***QNLOO-SPL*** [Kyoo · En · El · Oh · Oh · Es · Pee · El]
   The first official implementation paradigm of the Sovereign Value Protocol category, positioned as a "sovereign commercial value distribution framework with open source code." It is not a traditional free open-source license and does not seek OSI open-source certification. Its core institutions include the Dual-Asset Tiered Licensing Isolation Architecture, the Progressive Revenue-Based Tiered Authorization System, the Sovereign Statutory Digital Currency Dual-Channel Settlement Mechanism, the Distributed Socialized Evidence Collection & Tiered Qui Tam Bounty Sharing Mechanism, the Tiered and Fairly Estimated Liquidated Damages Rules, and Cross-Jurisdictional Judicial Adaptation.

7. **Dual-Asset Tiered Licensing Isolation Architecture (双资产分层许可隔离架构)** ***Shuang Zi Chan Fen Ceng Xu Ke Ge Li Jia Gou*** [Shwong · Dz · Chahn · Fun · Tsung · Shoo · Kuh · Guh · Lee · Jyah · Go]
   The core architectural design of QNLOO-SPL. It divides digital assets into two major categories — "computer source code assets" and "commercial operation/system logic/content assets" — each governed by different licensing rules. Source code continues to use Apache 2.0 to ensure open technological circulation, while commercial operations and creative assets are anchored to sovereign rules to ensure value distribution. This legally isolates the conflict between open-source and commercial fees, balancing the efficiency of technological dissemination with the recirculation of commercial value.


### Chapter 1 Value Mismatch

1. **Value Mismatch (价值错配)** ***Jia Zhi Cuo Pei*** [Jyah · Jrr · Tswaw · Pay]
   A state of industry where the subjects of value creation and the subjects of revenue capture are inconsistent. In the AI era, this manifests as open-source developers and content creators investing core costs, while commercial service providers capture the majority of operational revenue, breaking the two-way equilibrium of value exchange.

2. **Commercial Operation Behavior (商用运营行为)** ***Shang Yong Yun Ying Xing Wei*** [Shahng · Yohng · Yoon · Yeeng · Sheeng · Way]
   Conduct based on digital assets to provide commercial services and obtain operational revenue, including cloud API services, AI model commercial training, vertical industry solution development, etc. These fall within the domain of scenarios that the traditional copyright system cannot fully cover.

3. **Systematic Value Imbalance (系统性价值失衡)** ***Xi Tong Xing Jia Zhi Shi Heng*** [Shee · Tohng · Sheeng · Jyah · Jrr · Shrr · Huhng]
   Value distribution misalignment at the level of the entire ecosystem, manifested as costs being transmitted outward, revenue concentrating toward a few entities, leaving the value creation, consumption, and governance dimensions all as aggrieved parties, with only channel entities capturing excess returns — a non-sustainable state.

4. **Permissive OSS Value Collapse (开源价值坍缩)** ***Kai Yuan Jia Zhi Tan Suo*** [Kye · Ywen · Jyah · Jrr · Tahn · Swaw]
   The systemic imbalance in the AI era where the technological value created by open-source outputs is unidirectionally captured by cloud vendors and commercial service providers at near-zero marginal cost, while open-source developers gain only industry reputation but cannot recoup the high costs of R&D and computing power. This leads to the complete rupture of the value creation and revenue distribution chain, and the commercial self-circulation capacity of open-source projects is reduced to zero.

3. **SSDC Settlement Mechanism (主权法定数字货币双线结算机制)** ***Zhu Quan Fa Ding Shu Zi Huo Bi Shuang Xian Jie Suan Ji Zhi*** [Joo · Chwen · Fah · Ding · Shoo · Dz · Hwaw · Bee · Shwong · Shyen · Jyeh · Swahn · Jee · Jrr]

It is accompanied by an overseas transition period arrangement — during the three-year transition period, overseas commercial entities may temporarily use corporate cross-border payment conversion settlement; after the transition period expires, all must switch to the agreed SSDC smart contract settlement channel to establish territorial sovereign compliance.

Litigation standing adopts a territorial dual-track system, and the coordination rule adopts the Time-Priority Rule. The rights holder has zero advance payment, zero participation, and zero interference throughout.

5. **Tiered and Fairly Estimated Liquidated Damages Rules (梯度化公允清算违约金规则)** ***Ti Du Hua Gong Yun Qing Suan Wei Yue Jin Gui Ze*** [Tee · Doo · Hwah · Goong · Ywen · Cheeng · Swahn · Way · Yweh · Jeen · Gway · Dz]
   The default remedy institution of QNLOO-SPL. Two differentiated compensation standards are set based on the degree of fault — the Inadvertent Omission Tier applies a single-year 0.8% fee rate, with the premium allocated entirely to the relator; the Subjective Bad Faith Tier applies a 1.0% annual revenue fee rate with mandatory continuous liquidation for three years, with the first-year premium allocated to the relator and the subsequent two years' premium routed to the Ecological Defense Fund. Liquidated damages are characterized as "reasonably estimated fair compensation," with a 1.66x mathematical circuit-breaker mechanism, adapting to global judicial discretion standards.

7. **Big-Tech Progressive Revenue Penetration (大厂流水穿透)** ***Da Chang Liu Shui Chuan Tou*** [Dah · Chahng · Lyoo · Shway · Chwahn · Tow]
